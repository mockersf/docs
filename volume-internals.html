3.2.4.3 Volumes

Lifecycle

Volumes can be in one of 3 states; CREATING, CREATED, DESTROYING.

CREATING, volumes are still being initialized in BaggageClaim and are not yet ready to be used. CREATING, volumes can only transition to CREATED.

CREATED volumes are initialized in BaggageClaim and are ready to be used. CREATED volumes can only be transitioned to DESTROYING.

DESTROYING volumes are marked for removal from BaggageClaim, and should no longer be used; they will be removed from the database when they no longer exist in the BaggageClaim server.
